The Sensei :


Hello Everyone.. I am Soumen. Graduated from C.U. with specialization in Chemistry Hons.
Despite being in a normal bengali family and normal career which, everyvody wants to have, I always dreamt of learning martial arts. While working for a chemical company in Assam, I started my "training" on my own because that time I could not really afford a trainer. Started with some videos that I collected from a friend of mine. It was going good. Eventually I met a guy who was directly involved with a Karate School. You know as they say, "Desire anything from the core of your heart, keep your will power strong and you will get it.."
Then I never looked back. Now I am a Black Belt. I came back to Kolkata and left my job. Only with one goal in my mind."I want to teach martial arts to all and specially to them who desire to learn it but do not have the financial means."
All are welcome to my school. AGE IS NEVER A BARRIER IN THE PATH OF LEARING. Join Us.. Let's make a better India..
