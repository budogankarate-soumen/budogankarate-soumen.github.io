Course Content

This is the syllabus for all the 9 kyus. Devided according to the need and skill development for the students.

9th Kyu to 8th Kyu ( White to Yellow Belt)

Basics :

1. Move Forward in Zenkutsu dachi Oi-tsuki chudan.
2. Move Forward in Zenkutsu dachi Age-uke.
3. Move Forward in Zenkutsu dachi Soto-uke.
4. Move Forward in Z. D. Gedan Barai.
5. Move Forward in Z. D. Uchi - uke.
6. Move Forward in Z.D. Maegeri chudan.
Kata : NA
Kumite : NA

8th Kyu to 7th Kyu ( Yellow Belt to Green Belt)

Basics :

1. Move Forward in Z D Gyaku-tsuki Chudan.
2. Move Forward in Z D Gedan barai & Gyaku tsuki.
3. Move Forward in Z D Age uke & Gyaku tsuki.
4. Move Forward in Z D Soto uke & Gyaku tsuki.
5. Move Forward in Z D Uchi uke & Gyaku tsuki.
6. Move Forward in Z D Mae geri & Oi-tsuki chudan.
7. Move Forward in Z Yoko geri.
8. Move Forward Kokutsu dachi.
Kamae : Forward hand punch on the spot in 2 counts ( Kisami tsuki).

Kata : Tai kyo ku Shodan
Kumite : NA

7th Kyu to 6th Kyu ( Green Belt to Blue Belt)

Basics :

1. Move Forward in Z D Gedan barai Gyaku tsuki & Gedan barai.
2. Move Forward in Z D Age uke Gyaku tsuki & Age uke.
3. Move Forward in Z D Soto uke Gyaku tsuki & Soto uke.
4. Move Forward in Z D Uchi uke Gyaku tsuki & Uchi uke .
5. Move Forward in Z D Mae geri & Gyaku tsuki chudan.
6. Move Forward in Z D Yoko geri & Gyaku tsuki chudan.
7. Move Forward in Z D Mawashi geri.
8. Move Forward Kokutsu dachi Uchi uke.
9. Shuto uke in Heiko dachi.
Kamae:Reverse Punch(Gyaku tsuki) on the spot in 2 counts.
Kata : Tai kyo ku Sandan .
Kumite : NA

6th Kyu to 5th Kyu ( Blue Belt to Purple Belt)

Basics :

1. Move Forward in Zenkutsu dachi Oi-tsuki & Gyaku tsuki chudan.
2. Move Forward in Z D Gedan barai Gyaku tsuki & Age uke.
3. Move Forward in Z D Shoto uke Gyaku tsuki & Uchi uke .
4. Move Forward in Z D Mae geri & Gyaku tsuki chudan.
5. Move Forward in Z D Yoko geri & Gyaku tsuki chudan.
6. Move Forward in Z D Mawashi geri & Gyaku tsuki chudan.
7. Move Forward in Z D Ushiro geri.
8. Move Forward in kiba dachi Yoko geri Kekomi.
9. Move Forward Kokutsu dachi Shuto uke.
10. Move Forward Ashibarai.
Kamae:Forward hand punch & reverse punch (on the spot in 2 counts).
Oi-tsuki & Gyaku tsuki

Kata : Heian Shodan .
Kumite : Ippon kumite ( Jodan tsuki, chudan tsuki & mae geri chudan) .
Sparring: 2 Minutes. ( Jiyu kumite)

5th Kyu to 4th Kyu ( Purple Belt to Brown 4th)

Basics :

1. Move Forward in Zenkutsu dachi Oi-tsuki & Gyaku tsuki chudan.
2. Move Forward in Z D Gedan barai , Age uke & Gyaku tsuki .
3. Move Forward in Z D Shoto uke, Uchi uke & Gyaku tsuki .
4. Move Forward in Z D moroto uke.
5. Move Forward in Z D Yoko geri & Gyaku tsuki chudan.
6. Move Forward in Z D Mawashi geri & Gyaku tsuki chudan.
7. Move Forward in Z D Ushiro geri.
8. Move Forward in kiba dachi Yoko geri Keage.
9. Move Forward Ashibarai & Gyaku tsuki chudan.
10. Uraken on the spot.
Kata : Heian Nidan .
Kumite : Gohon kumite: 5 Steps ( Jodan tsuki, Chudan tsuki,Mae geri,Mawashi geri, Yoko geri).
Sparring: 2 Minutes. ( Jiyu kumite).

4th Kyu to 3rd Kyu ( Brown 4th to Brown 3rd)

Basics :

1. Move Forward in Zenkutsu dachi Samban tsuki chudan.
2. Move Forward in Z D Gedan barai Gyaku tsuki & Age uke Gyaku tsuki .
3. Move Forward in Z D Shoto uke Gyaku tsuki & Uchi uke Gyaku tsuki .
4. Move Forward in Z D Uchi uke Oi-tsuki & Gyaku tsuki.
5. Move Forward in Kokutsu Dachi Kakiwake Uke.
6. Move Forward Ashibarai Oi-tsuki & Gyaku tsuki chudan.
7. Move Forward in Z D Mae geri , Gedan Barai & Gyaku tsuki chudan.
8. Move Forward in Z D Yoko geri , Uraken & Gyaku tsuki chudan.
9. Move Forward in Z D Mawashi geri , Uchi uke & Gyaku tsuki chudan.
10. Move Forward in Z D Ushiro geri & Gyaku tsuki chudan.
11. Kakkato Mawashi geri - on the spot.
Kata : Heian Sandan .
Kumite : Sambon kumite ( Jodan tsuki, Chudan tsuki,Mae geri).
Sparring: 3 Minutes. ( Jiyu kumite).

3rd Kyu to 2nd Kyu ( Brown 3rd to Brown 2nd)

Basics :

1. Move Forward in Zenkutsu dachi Samban tsuki ( Face level & double middle punch).
2. Move Forward in Z D Gedan barai , Age uke, Uchi uke & Gyaku tsuki .
3. Move Forward in Z D Shoto uke,Uchi uke ,Gedan barai & Gyaku tsuki .
4. Move Forward in Z D manjo uke.
5. Move Forward Mae geri, Mawashi geri & Gyaku tsuki chudan.
6. Move Forward Yoko geri, yokogeri kekomi & Gyaku tsuki chudan.
7. Move Forward in Z D Ashibarai, Mawashigeri & Gyaku tsuki chudan.
8. Move Forward in Z D Ushiro geri , Ake uke & Gyaku tsuki chudan.
9. Kamae: Kizami tsuki & Gyaku tsuki ( slide forward).
10.Move Forward Kakkato Mawashi geri.
Kata : Heian Yondan .
Kumite : Ippon kumite- 6 counts ( Jodan tsuki, Chudan tsuki,Mae geri,Mawashi geri, yoko geri , ushiro geri) .
Sparring: 3 Minutes. ( Jiyu kumite).

2nd Kyu to 1st Kyu (Brown 2nd to Brown 1st)

Basics :

1. Move Forward in Z D Gedan barai Gyaku tsuki , Age uke Gyaku tsuki & Uchi uke Gyaku tsuki.
2. Move Forward in Z D Shoto uke Gyaku tsuki ,Uchi uke Gyaku tsuki & Gedan barai Gyaku tsuki .
3. Move Forward Kokutsu dachi Uchi Uke, Z D Kizami tsuki Jodan & Gyaku tsuki.
4. Move Forward Kizami Mae geri, Mawashi geri , Kizami tsuki & Gyaku tsuki .
5. Move Forward Mawashi geri, Ushiro geri & Gyaku tsuki chudan.
6. Move Forward stepping Kakkato Mawashi geri.
7. Move Forward stepping Ushiro geri.
8. Move Forward Mae geri, Yoko geri ,Mawashi geri & Ushiro geri.
9. Move Forward Mawashi geri, Kakkato Mawashi geri & Mawashi geri.
Kata : Heian Godan & Tekki Shodan.
Kumite : Jiyu Ippon kumite (with fighting stance) .
Sparring: 3 Minutes. ( Jiyu kumite).

BROWN 1ST TO SHODAN BLACK BELT

Basics :

1. Move Forward in Z D Samban tsuki – one face level & double middle level.
2. Move Forward in Z D Gedan barai Gyaku tsuki & Gedan barai.
3. Move Forward in Z D Ake uke, Mae geri, Gyaku tsuki & Gedan barai .
4. Move Forward Outer forearm block change front leg into Kiba Dachi with elbow strike and back fist, change front leg into forward stance with reverse punch and lower block.
(Z D Shoto uke, kiba dachi empi uchi, uraken, Z D gyaku tsuki & gedan barai ) .
5. Move Forward Inner forearm block, forward hand punch, reverse punch and lower block.
( Z D Uchi uki, Kizami tsuki jodan, gyaku tsuki & gedan barai).
6. Move Forward in Back stance with knife hand block and front kick with front leg and put the leg in forward stance, reverse finger thrust and lower block.
(Kokutsu dachi shuto uke, kizami mae geri,gyaku nukite & gedan barai).
7. Kamae: Front kick with front leg & put the leg down, front kick with back leg & put the leg in forward stance, forward hand punch & reverse punch, kamae.
( Kamae – Kizami mae geri, maegeri, Z D kizami tsuki & gyaku tsuki).
8. Kamae: Round house kick with front leg & put the leg down & round house kick with back leg, back fist and reverse punch, kamae.
( Kamae : Kizami mawashi geri, mawashi geri, uraken and gyaku tsuki – kamae).
9. Kamae: Side kick with front leg & put the leg down, side kick with back leg put the leg down in kibadachi, back fist and reverse punch in forward stance-kamae.
(Kizami yoko geri, yoko geri, kiba dachi urakken & gyaku tsuki-kamae).
10. Kamae: Front kick with back leg & withdraw the leg into back and back kick with same back leg , put the leg in front in forward stance, lower block and reverse punch-kamae.
( Mae geri, Ushiro geri, gedan barai & gyaku tsuki).
11. The following kicks with 4 counts: Front Kick, Side Kick, Round house Kick & Back Kick.
12. Jumping Spinning hook ( Tobi Kakkato Mawashi Geri).
Kata : Heian Shodan, Heian Nidan, Heian Sandan, Heian Yondan, Heian Godan ,Tekki Shodan, Bassai Dai .
Kumite : Gohon Kumite, Sambon Kumite, Ippon Kumite, Jiyu Ippon kumite & Ippon ( 12 counts) .
Sparring: 3 Minutes. ( Jiyu kumite).
