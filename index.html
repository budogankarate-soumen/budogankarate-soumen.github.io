WHY do we want to learn karate

Martial Arts. The ultimate self-knowledge.

“The martial arts are ultimately self-knowledge. A punch or a kick is not to knock the hell out of the guy in front, but to knock the hell out of your ego, your fear, or your hang-ups.” - Bruce Lee

"Defense is the best offence !"

Self defense classes will make you more aware of your surroundings. You’re never planning to be attacked, but your attacker is the one with the plan. Self defense classes will help you to be aware at all times and ready, should this type of situation arise. You might be shocked for a second, but you will have the necessary reactions to protect yourself.

Give your child a better future.

Whether your kid is too bossy, too shy, or perhaps just a little hyper, the martial arts can help your child learn many important life lessons. Your child will learn that confidence and respect for others comes from a deep sense of self-knowledge.

About the School

The School originally named as, "Behala AdarshaNagar BudoGan Karate Academy(Kolkata)" is an attempt to teach and make everyone aware about Karate. The founder and the Sensei is Soumen Das , a dedicated learner himself. He became a black belt holder from a zero-level guy, who had absolutley no skills about Karate. A true example of dedication and self-taught personality.
We are an young organization operated by a group of youngsters to whom, wellfare of the human beings really matters. It is not always about the money, but about the passion,it is about making story. Those are realted to this organization and taking lessons from it, we want them to be more proficient, more successful in their lives.
